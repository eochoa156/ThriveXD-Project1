Game Rules

Tic Tac Toe:

  1. Player starts as X while the computer plays as Os
  2. First to get 3 in a row diagonally, horizontally, or vertically wins
  3. Be warned, the computer is a tough opponent!


Simon:

  1. First, the computer goes, then the player
  2. The player must press the corresponding button that matches the one the computer pressed
  3. Try to score as high as you can!
